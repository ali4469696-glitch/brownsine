Our Philosophy

Craftsmanship is
Our Heritage

For over three decades, Lysome has occupied a singular position in the world of luxury furniture. Born in a small atelier in Northern Italy, we have grown into a global name — yet every sofa we create still bears the mark of a human hand.

We source only from suppliers who share our obsession with quality: ancient oak forests for our frames, heritage tanneries for our leathers, and master weavers for our velvets and linens.
